Our Company

White&Trust Pharmaceutical (INDIA) PVT.LTD is committed to assembling a dedicated and skilled team of professionals to offer customers the best solutions tailored to their needs. Our collaborative approach, backed by a robust quality management system, has positioned White&Trust Pharmaceutical (INDIA) PVT.LTD as a reputable name in the clinical laboratory products industry.

As one of India's fastest-growing diagnostic companies,we take pride in our state-of-the-art manufacturing setup, ensuring consistent quality at highly competitive prices. With extensive experience in the diagnostics sector, we provide cutting-edge products, including semi-auto analyzers, biochemistry analyzers, dry bath incubators, ELISA strip readers, and ELISA plate washers.

Our unwavering dedication drives us to continually enhance our products, positively impacting public health and exceeding customer expectations. By refining our manufacturing processes for greater efficiency and strengthening our Research & Development division, we ensure that White&Trust Pharmaceutical (INDIA) PVT.LTD launches high-quality innovations in the industry year after year.

Our Mission

Our primary objective is to deliver top-quality instruments with enhanced functionality, driving us toward our ultimate goal of **"Customer Satisfaction."** We uphold exceptional quality standards for our valued clients by ensuring that all audit reports align with our stringent benchmarks.

Our Vision

At White&Trust, we continuously motivate our team to take initiative and embrace fresh ideas, enabling us to adopt and integrate advanced technologies. Our relentless efforts focus on expanding our portfolio with innovative products, ensuring we stay ahead in the industry.

Quality standard

At White & Trust, we prioritize Excellence, Swift Service, and Strong Customer Relations. Our core values lie in delivering top-quality instruments on time, adhering to customer specifications with utmost care and precaution before dispatch.

With an extensive customer base across India, White & Trust has earned a solid reputation in the industry for its dedication to providing world-class products and services at competitive rates, fulfilling the diverse requirements of our clients.
